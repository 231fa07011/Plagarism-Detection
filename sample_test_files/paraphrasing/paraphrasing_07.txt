Body 1: Rented power - whether it's for simple file storage or complex math tasks - allows a business to only pay for what they use right now, rather than spending huge amounts of money on their own servers.

Body 2: New ways of building websites that use 'containers' allow developers to update their apps many times a day without causing any crashes for the people visiting the site.

Data: Reports show that over 90% of modern companies now use at least one of the big web-power providers for most of their daily work and customer services.
